Model Architecture
i
Typhon Multi-Model Engine: Runs three complementary algorithms in parallel — (1) SEIRD: Susceptible → Exposed → Infectious → Recovered / Deceased compartmental model with Euler integration; (2) SEIRS: same as SEIRD but with waning immunity re-introduction to the susceptible pool; (3) Network-adjusted SIR: demographic contact matrix applied to modulate transmission by age stratum. Results are fused using a weighted ensemble. Demographic multipliers (population density, urban fraction, mobility), NPI-modified beta, vaccine-reduced susceptibility, and healthcare-capacity-scaled mortality are applied across all models. Rₜ is estimated post-hoc from the ratio of lagged new-infection windows.
